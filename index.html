About

I am originally from Milwaukee Wisconsin where I was born and raised. I moved to Portland Oregon in 2016, after I graduated from Cardinal Strich University with a BA in Business. I visited Portland the summer before I graduated and fell in love with the city. What caught my attention was the easy access to the surrounding nature. I get the benefits of living in a city but also have the pleasure of hiking snowboarding, and surfing.

My decision to enroll in Epicodus was my interest in programming. I started hearing about coding boot camps while I was in college. I decided to take a html course on Codecademy. It sparked my interest and I enjoyed the idea of learning a new skill. After moving to Portland I had a hard time finding in a job in my field of study so I looked in to coding boot camps. Epicodus had the best reviews in the country and happened to be fifteen minutes from my house. I decided to enroll and have not looked back.
